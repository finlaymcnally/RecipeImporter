## Problem statement

QualitySuite live ETA was only modeling currently active experiments. With queued experiments waiting behind the worker cap, ETA appeared dramatically too low for most of the run.

## Acceptance criteria

- Live ETA keeps using active scheduler telemetry when available.
- ETA includes queued experiments beyond currently active workers.
- If active ETA samples are missing, ETA can fall back to completed-experiment median duration.
- Focused ETA and quality-run test slices pass.

## Constraints/gotchas and decision

- ETA remains a heuristic and can still drift with heterogeneous experiment costs.
- Decision: prefer queue-aware and slightly conservative estimates over lowballing by ignoring queued work.
