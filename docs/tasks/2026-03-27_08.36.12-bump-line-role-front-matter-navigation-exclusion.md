# Bump Line-Role Front-Matter Navigation Exclusion

## Problem

The line-role stage was correctly demoting front-matter chapter-taxonomy headings to `OTHER`, but many of those rows were still staying review-eligible instead of getting excluded upstream.
That meant obvious contents/front-matter heading runs such as `The Four Elements of Good Cooking / SALT / What is Salt? / How Salt Works` could still survive into the knowledge packet queue and mix with real explanatory prose.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Outside-recipe chapter-taxonomy heading runs in clear front-matter/navigation clusters can receive `review_exclusion_reason="navigation"`.
- The same headings must still stay reviewable when nearby explanatory prose shows a real lesson block instead of a contents/menu cluster.
- Focused parsing regressions cover both the exclusion case and the preserved heading-plus-prose case.

## Verification

- `. .venv/bin/activate && pytest tests/parsing/test_canonical_line_roles.py -q`
- `. .venv/bin/activate && pytest tests/staging/test_import_session.py -q`

## Decision

Keep the change narrow and local.
Do not invent new knowledge packet types yet; first push more obvious front-matter/title-list junk out of the queue at line-role time, then dial back if the policy starts dropping real lesson headings.
