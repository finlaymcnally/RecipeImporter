## Problem statement
Spinner ETA averages lag behind current throughput after rate changes, making estimates feel stale.

## Acceptance criteria
- Shared ETA helper incorporates stronger recency bias.
- ETA behavior applies uniformly to all spinner/status flows that use `_run_with_progress_status`.
- Tests cover the new recency behavior.

## Verification steps
- `source .venv/bin/activate && pytest -q tests/labelstudio/test_labelstudio_benchmark_helpers_progress.py -k "recent_rate_average_seconds_per_task or shows_eta_for_xy_progress or shows_eta_for_canonical_line_role_progress"`

## Evidence
- Updated rate helper in `cookimport/cli.py` to blend most-recent step timing with weighted history.
- Added/updated tests proving recency bias and slowdown responsiveness.

## Constraints / decision
- Chose a deterministic 50/50 blend between newest per-step duration and weighted window average to improve responsiveness without removing smoothing.

## Rollback / compensation
- Revert `_recent_rate_average_seconds_per_task` to weighted-only output and restore prior test expectations.
