# Interactive benchmark upload confirmation removal

Finding:
- The upload branch in `_interactive_mode` already represents explicit user intent (`upload` mode chosen, or upload fallback when eval-only artifacts are missing).
- A second `questionary.confirm(...)` added redundant friction and could feel contradictory in normal benchmark flows.

Decision:
- Remove the extra upload confirmation prompt.
- Keep credential resolution unchanged (`_resolve_interactive_labelstudio_settings`) before calling `labelstudio_benchmark(...)`.
