# Knowledge Preview Fan-Out Comes From Seed Non-Recipe Chunks

Observed on `2026-03-16_20.23.25` hypothetical codex-shaped preview:

- Stage 7 produced `389` non-recipe spans (`182` knowledge, `207` other) across `1249` outside-recipe blocks.
- Prompt preview passed all seed non-recipe spans into `build_knowledge_jobs(...)`, not just seed `knowledge_spans`.
- `build_knowledge_jobs(...)` wrote one job per chunk returned by `chunks_from_non_recipe_blocks(...)`, not one job per span.
- That yielded `409` total chunks:
  - `324` written jobs
  - `85` skipped chunks, all `lane=noise`
- Every written job had `heuristics.suggested_lane = knowledge`.

Why so many chunks survive:

- `chunks_from_non_recipe_blocks(...)` fragments spans on headings, callouts, format changes, and max-size boundaries.
- The chunk lane scorer in `chunks.py` defaults many borderline chunks to `knowledge` when they are not strong noise.
- `build_knowledge_jobs(...)` only skips `noise` by default (`skip_suggested_lanes=("noise",)`), so non-noise chunks all become prompt jobs.

Practical implication:

- After recipe-span cleanup reduces bogus recipes, more front matter / explanatory prose stays outside recipes.
- Under the current refinement contract, that enlarged outside-recipe surface is exactly what the hypothetical knowledge preview will try to review.
