## Findings
- The displayed single-profile status panel is rendered by `_run_with_progress_status` -> `_format_boxed_progress` in `cookimport/cli.py`.
- Clipping came from two places at once:
  - panel body lines were always hard-truncated to panel width (`...`), and
  - `_AllMethodProgressDashboard.set_task(...)` hard-capped task strings at 180 chars before panel rendering.
- The 92-column max panel cap further increased truncation on wide terminals.

## Seam Applied
- Keep the boxed panel contract, but wrap body lines within panel width instead of truncating every long line.
- Raise the panel max width cap to better use terminal width while preserving width clamp safety.
- Remove the 180-char task clamp so single-profile task context can flow through to wrapped panel rows.
- Switch live benchmark spinner to a larger `bouncingBar` style for better visual prominence.
