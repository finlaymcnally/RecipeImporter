## Discovery

- ETA was based only on a weighted window of recent step durations.
- In sharp throughput changes, this smoothed average lagged and updated slower than operators expected.

## Change

- Keep weighted recent history (`30/20/20/20/10`, newest first).
- Blend in the most-recent per-step duration at 50% weight.
- Shared helper `_recent_rate_average_seconds_per_task(...)` now returns:
  - `0.5 * latest_step_seconds + 0.5 * weighted_recent_average`

## Effect

- ETA/avg strings in all spinner paths react faster to new observed processing rates while still keeping smoothing from history.
