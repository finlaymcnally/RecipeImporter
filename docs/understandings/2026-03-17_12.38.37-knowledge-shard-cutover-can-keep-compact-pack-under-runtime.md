# Knowledge Shard Cutover Can Keep The Compact Pack Under The Runtime

- The durable runtime cutover for knowledge does not require a brand-new Codex pipeline pack on day one.
- The useful split is: deterministic chunking and shard planning in repo code, bounded worker execution in `phase_worker_runtime.py`, strict shard validation in `codex_farm_knowledge_ingest.py`, and compatibility copies of validated payloads back into `raw/llm/<slug>/knowledge/{in,out}` for older prompt/debug readers.
- That keeps `llm_knowledge_pipeline=codex-knowledge-shard-v1` honest at the product/runtime layer while still reusing `recipe.knowledge.compact.v1` as the current pack id underneath.
- The important authority boundary is shard ownership plus block-surface validation, not whether the payload still looks like the older compact bundle shape.
