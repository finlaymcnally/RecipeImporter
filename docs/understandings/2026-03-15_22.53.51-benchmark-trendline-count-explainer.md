---
summary: "Benchmark trend chart line count depends on quick filters and derived benchmark variants, not just selected metrics."
read_when:
  - When debugging why Previous Runs benchmark trend lines increased unexpectedly
  - When changing benchmark trend series grouping or quick-filter defaults
---

`Previous Runs` still defaults to `official_full_golden_only: true`, but persisted dashboard UI state can override that. When the filter is off, the trend chart includes unofficial benchmark rows and groups them by `benchmarkVariantForRecord(...)`. With the current history data that means `vanilla`, `codexfarm`, and a large `other` bucket, so two selected score metrics produce six rolling trend lines instead of four.
