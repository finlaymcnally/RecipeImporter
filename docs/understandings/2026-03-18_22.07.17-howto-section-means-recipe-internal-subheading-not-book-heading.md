# HOWTO_SECTION Meaning

`HOWTO_SECTION` has a misleading name. In this repo it should mean recipe-internal subsection structure only.

Use it for headings that split one recipe into component ingredient lists or method families, for example:

- `FOR THE SAUCE`
- `FOR THE DRESSING`
- `TO FINISH`
- `FOR SERVING`

Do not use it for generic cookbook/topic headings such as:

- `Salt and Pepper`
- `Cooking Acids`
- `Starches`
- `Stewing and Braising`

Those headings should usually be `KNOWLEDGE` or `OTHER` depending on whether they introduce explanatory content or just book/navigation structure.
