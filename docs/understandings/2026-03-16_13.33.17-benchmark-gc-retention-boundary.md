`cookimport bench gc` is benchmark-only retention, not a general `data/output` sweeper.

Useful aggressive cleanup command for this repo:

`cookimport bench gc --keep-full-runs 0 --keep-full-days 0 --include-labelstudio-benchmark --prune-benchmark-processed-outputs --apply`

What it does:
- prunes durable benchmark roots under `data/golden/bench/*` and optionally `data/golden/benchmark-vs-golden/*`
- can also prune matching processed-output roots under `data/output/<run_id>/`
- leaves `performance_history.csv` alone, so dashboard/history stats remain
- refuses to delete roots when durable CSV history cannot be confirmed
