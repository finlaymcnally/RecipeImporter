---
summary: "RecipeImport must force its Codex home at the CodexFarm runner layer because its local pipeline pack does not declare `codex_home_profile`."
read_when:
  - "When changing how RecipeImport chooses the Codex home for CodexFarm subprocesses"
  - "When CodexFarm runs launched from RecipeImport unexpectedly use the wrong Codex session/profile"
---

- RecipeImport's local `llm_pipelines/pipelines/*.json` pack files do not declare CodexFarm `codex_home_profile` fields.
- Because of that, setting only `CODEX_FARM_CODEX_HOME_RECIPE` on the parent shell is not enough to make the pack self-describe the desired Codex home.
- The reliable enforcement point is `cookimport/llm/codex_farm_runner.py`, because all RecipeImport-owned CodexFarm subprocesses pass through `_merge_env(...)`.
- Injecting `CODEX_HOME=~/.codex-recipe` there also covers helper calls like `codex-farm models list`, `pipelines list`, `run errors`, and `run autotune`, not just the main `process` call.
