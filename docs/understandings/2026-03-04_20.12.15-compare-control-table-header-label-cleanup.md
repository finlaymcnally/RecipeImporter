- The `(<field_key>)` suffix came from `analysisFieldLabel`, which was reused across compare/control dropdowns, chart labels, and group-table headers.
- Numeric markers (`[numeric]`) in compare/split dropdowns were appended when options were rendered, independent of field-label generation.
- Header height/wrapping is easiest to enforce at `.compare-control-groups-table th` with wrap + fixed two-line height styles.
