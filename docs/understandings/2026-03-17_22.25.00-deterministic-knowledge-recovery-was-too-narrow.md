Deterministic outside-recipe `KNOWLEDGE` recovery in `cookimport/parsing/canonical_line_roles.py` had become too narrow in three specific ways:

- knowledge prose effectively required long prose shape, so short explanatory fragments like `acid, which brightens and balances` never recovered
- knowledge headings mostly depended on uppercase compact-heading shape or neighbor evidence, so title-case pedagogical headings like `How Salt Works` and `Using Salt` fell through
- attribution/endorsement credit lines like `-Alice Waters ... bestselling author ...` had no deterministic recovery path and defaulted to `OTHER`

The practical effect was the March 17 vanilla crater: many outside-recipe rows became `deterministic_unresolved` plus `deterministic_unavailable`, then `_fallback_prediction(...)` forced `OTHER`.

The fix was to widen deterministic knowledge recovery itself, not the fallback:

- allow short domain-plus-explanation fragments to count as knowledge prose
- allow obvious title-case pedagogical/domain headings to count as knowledge headings even without neighbor evidence
- allow endorsement-credit lines to count as outside-recipe knowledge prose

This restores the missing deterministic `KNOWLEDGE` path without reintroducing the deleted post-Codex rollback/veto layer.
