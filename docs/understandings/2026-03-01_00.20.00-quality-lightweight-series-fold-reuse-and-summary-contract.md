# Quality Lightweight Series Fold Reuse And Summary Contract

- The lightweight series can stay as orchestration only: each fold writes `suite.json` plus `experiments_effective.json`, then calls existing `run_quality_suite(...)` and reads `summary.json` rows back.
- Winner selection and interaction smoke decisions are derived exclusively from per-experiment fold summary means (`practical_f1_macro`, `strict_f1_macro`, `source_success_rate`) and deltas, so no new scoring engine is introduced.
- Resume safety is handled at two layers:
  - series-level compatibility check (`lightweight_series_resolved.json` hashes for profile/experiments/thresholds + baseline id),
  - fold-level reuse via `fold_result.json` and optional quality-run partial resume inside `quality_runs/`.
