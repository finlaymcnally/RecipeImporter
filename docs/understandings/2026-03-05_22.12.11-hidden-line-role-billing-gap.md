Follow-up on hidden line-role spend vs billing truth:

- The `~1.6M` estimate is a repo-backed estimate from saved line-role prompt and response files only.
- That is best treated as a lower-to-mid bound for retained artifacts, not as an exact billing number.

  - token meters can include wrapper/system scaffolding not preserved in `prompt_*.txt`
  - cached-input / request-accounting semantics do not map 1:1 to saved text bytes
  - retries or duplicate attempts may be underrepresented by retained prompt logs
- For the retained March 4 QualitySuite contamination alone, a more realistic billing-truth band is about 1.7M to 2.5M tokens.
- Best revised midpoint for that retained hidden QualitySuite slice: about 2.0M to 2.2M tokens.
