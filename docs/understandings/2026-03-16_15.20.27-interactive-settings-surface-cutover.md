The interactive CLI now has two coordinated surfaces.

- `choose_run_settings(...)` owns per-run top-tier decisions: recipe Codex on/off, benchmark-only line-role and knowledge toggles, and per-run Codex model/reasoning overrides.
- The persistent `Settings` screen owns saved operator defaults: workers/scheduler knobs, EPUB plus PDF OCR defaults, webschema defaults, recipe/knowledge/tags defaults, Codex command/path/model/context defaults, tag-catalog wiring, output/split sizing, and Label Studio credentials.

Important boundary:

- The persistent `Settings` screen is for saved operator defaults, not for benchmark-lab/internal parser or scoring seams, and not for hidden compatibility pipeline-id fields.
