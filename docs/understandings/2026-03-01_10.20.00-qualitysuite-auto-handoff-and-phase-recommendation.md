# QualitySuite Auto-Handoff Heuristic

- Phase B auto-handoff now reads prior `summary.json` (and optional `folds.json`) via `--auto-candidates-from-summary` or `--auto-candidates-from-latest-in`.
- Selection precedence is: explicit `--candidate-experiment-id` first, auto-candidate mode second, thresholds/default candidates last.
- Heuristic behavior:
  - selects one candidate when top-ranked candidate is winner or tied-top on all evaluated unique folds and at least two unique folds are available;
  - selects two candidates when top-two mean practical deltas are within `0.003`;
  - otherwise falls back to top-ranked candidate with warning metadata for sparse/noisy evidence.
- The same heuristic now writes `phase_a_promotion_recommendation` into tournament `summary.json` and `report.md`.
- Seed precedence now supports explicit `--seed`/`--seed-list` combined with `--max-seeds` (dedupe explicit sequence first, then cap).
