# Detached Benchmark Oracle Runs Need A Second Local Worker To Finalize Turn 1 And Launch Turn 2

- The detached benchmark uploader previously stopped after spawning Oracle turn 1. It wrote `oracle_upload_status.json` once near launch time, so completed runs could still look `running`, and nothing consumed the saved Oracle answer to trigger `cookimport bench oracle-followup`.
- The fix is a second detached local worker rooted in the same source run directory. That worker waits for the turn-1 log to become terminal, refreshes `oracle_upload_status.json` from the completed log plus Oracle session metadata, and only then decides whether to auto-launch turn 2.
- The worker also needs its own tiny artifact surface for observability. `oracle_auto_followup.json` records whether turn 2 was skipped, timed out, or launched successfully, and `oracle_auto_followup.log` captures the worker's local progress and any bridge failure text.
