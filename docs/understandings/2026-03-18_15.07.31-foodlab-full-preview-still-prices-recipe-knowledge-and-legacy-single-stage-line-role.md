# Full Preview Uses Mixed Surfaces

The saved Food Lab full prompt preview currently prices three surfaces:

- `recipe_llm_correct_and_link`
- `extract_knowledge_optional`
- `line_role`

Recipe and knowledge reflect their current preview builders, but line-role in that mixed report is still the older single-stage reconstruction (`line-role.canonical.v1`) rather than the newer dedicated two-phase file-backed preview shape. That means the recipe and knowledge costs from the mixed report are still useful, but line-role from that same report is not directly comparable to the newer two-phase line-role-only preview.

For the current Food Lab mixed preview run (`data/output/2026-03-18_15.05.00-foodlab-full-preview`), the useful non-line-role stage totals are:

- recipe correction: about `31.9k` estimated total tokens across 5 calls
- knowledge harvest: about `261.7k` estimated total tokens across 5 calls

Knowledge is therefore far more expensive than recipe on this saved run, but still much cheaper than the separate two-phase line-role preview.
