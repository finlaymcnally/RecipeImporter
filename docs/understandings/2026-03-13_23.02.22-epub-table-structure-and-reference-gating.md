read_when:
  - When changing EPUB table extraction or table-aware chunking
  - When debugging why Food Lab conversion pages do or do not land under `tables/`
---

- `unstructured.partition.html` emits `Table` elements whose metadata includes `text_as_html`; parsing that HTML gives stable `<tr>` rows, preserves empty cells, and avoids trying to infer columns from flattened table text.
- Preserving row structure alone was not enough for Food Lab: `WEIGHT CONVERSIONS` and `COMMON TEMPERATURE CONVERSIONS` were still being scored as recipe candidates, so `tables.py` never saw them in `nonRecipeBlocks`.
- A narrow recipe-likeness penalty on obvious reference-section titles fixed that second bottleneck. Before the penalty, the Food Lab stage run at `data/output/2026-03-13_22.59.32` wrote 3 unrelated tables. After the penalty, the rerun at `data/output/2026-03-13_23.01.23` wrote 6 tables including the three conversion/reference targets.
