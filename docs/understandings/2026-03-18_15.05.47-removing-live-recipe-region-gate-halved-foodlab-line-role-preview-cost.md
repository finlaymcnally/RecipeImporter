# Removing Live Gate Halved Food Lab Preview Cost

The two-phase file-backed prototype proved the transport, but it was still paying to reread the same book rows twice. On `thefoodlabcutdown`, the predictive preview under `data/output/2026-03-18_15.15.00-foodlab-file-preview/` estimated:

- `10` line-role calls
- `~1,303,803` total tokens

After removing the live `recipe_region_gate` pass and restoring one file-backed `line_role` phase, the predictive preview under `data/output/2026-03-18_15.36.00-foodlab-single-phase-preview/` estimated:

- `5` line-role calls
- `~636,852` total tokens

So the gate removal saved `666,951` estimated tokens on Food Lab while preserving the file-backed transport. The remaining cost is still dominated by the single-pass task files themselves (`~2.51M` task-payload chars across 5 shards), not by the wrapper prompts.
