- `cookimport/config/codex_decision.py` is the practical seam: it owns top-tier profile patches, paired benchmark Codex/vanilla contracts, surface classification, command-boundary decisions, and the `codex_decision_*` metadata stamped into run-config payloads.

- The direct boundaries are enforced in `cookimport/cli.py` for `stage`, `labelstudio-import`, `labelstudio-benchmark`, `bench speed-run`, and `bench quality-run`, plus `cookimport/entrypoint.py` for the `import` shortcut.

- Prediction-run persistence needed a second hook in `cookimport/labelstudio/ingest.py`; otherwise the CLI could validate correctly but the saved run-config inside prediction manifests would still miss the decision metadata.

- Analytics needed one careful fix: `line_role_pipeline=deterministic-v1` must stay `deterministic`, not `line_role_only`. The safe way was to reuse the shared classifier and only pass keys that are actually present.
