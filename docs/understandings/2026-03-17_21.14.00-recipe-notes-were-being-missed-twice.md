Two fixes were required.

1. Deterministic line-role needed an explicit `RECIPE_NOTES` path for short note patterns:
- `Store leftover...`
- `Refrigerate leftovers...`
- `Cover and refrigerate leftovers...`
- leading `Ideal for ...` serving/use suggestions

Without that, those rows fell into `OTHER`, `INSTRUCTION_LINE`, or neighbor-driven `KNOWLEDGE`.

2. Even after deterministic labeling improved, `_apply_nonrecipe_authority_to_predictions(...)` in `cookimport/labelstudio/ingest.py` was still rewriting every outside-recipe line to binary `KNOWLEDGE` or `OTHER`. That clobbered real outside-recipe `RECIPE_NOTES` despite the intended contract that final non-recipe authority is only a binary seam.

The correct behavior is:
- deterministic line-role may emit `RECIPE_NOTES` outside recipe spans when the text is clearly a recipe-tail note
- final non-recipe authority may only arbitrate rows already labeled `OTHER` or `KNOWLEDGE`
- recipe-structural labels such as `RECIPE_NOTES` must survive that projection unchanged
