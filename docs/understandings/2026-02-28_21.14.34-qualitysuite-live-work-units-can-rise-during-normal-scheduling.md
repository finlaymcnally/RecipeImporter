# QualitySuite Live Work Units Can Rise During Normal Scheduling

- Live `work_units` in QualitySuite comes from `estimate_quality_run_eta(...)` and is the sum of weighted scheduler counters (`pending`, `active`, `evaluate_active`, `wing_backlog`, `post_active`, `prep_active`), not a strict count of remaining configs.
- `pending` has weight `1.0` while other active states have lower weights, so transitions between scheduler phases can make the weighted total move up or down even while progress is real.
- The estimator only sums currently active experiments. As queued experiments are admitted, their remaining units are added, which can temporarily increase displayed `work_units`.
- Race mode writes separate scheduler timeseries per round (probe/mid/final); when a new round starts with a larger queue than the prior round just finished, an experiment's chosen remaining-units snapshot can jump.
- Timeout recovery and retry paths can also requeue configs, increasing per-experiment `pending` in timeseries and causing visible `work_units` upticks.
