# Line-role score purge boundaries

- The label-first seam is now reason-only: `CanonicalLineRolePrediction`, `AuthoritativeLabeledLine`, `AuthoritativeBlockLabel`, and `RecipeSpan` keep labels, provenance, and `escalation_reasons`, but no `confidence`, `trust_score`, or `escalation_score`.
- Codex escalation is now driven by explicit reasons (`deterministic_unresolved`, fallback/sanitization/veto/disagreement) and never by numeric thresholds.
- Reviewer/export surfaces changed in lockstep: `analysis.line_role_escalation` replaces `analysis.line_role_trust`, and `analysis.explicit_escalation_changed_lines_packet` replaces the old low-trust packet.
