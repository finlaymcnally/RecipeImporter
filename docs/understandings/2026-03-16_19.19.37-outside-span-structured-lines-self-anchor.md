  - "When titleless pseudo-recipes keep appearing even though grouping already rejects missing-title spans."
---

Before this fix, `_outside_span_structured_line_allowed(...)` called `_outside_span_has_neighboring_recipe_evidence(...)`, and that helper treated neighboring `ingredient_like` / `instruction_like` lines as sufficient proof. In practice that let small outside-span clusters self-justify: one ingredient line plus one instruction line could both keep their structured labels without any nearby title-like anchor, yield/howto line, or importer-held `within_recipe_span` context.

The implemented tightening keeps the title/variant path unchanged, but makes outside-span `INGREDIENT_LINE` and `INSTRUCTION_LINE` require nearby anchor evidence instead: a nearby title-like line, yield/howto/variant boundary, or a neighboring line already inside an importer-held recipe span. That preserves the grouping hard gate as the final correctness boundary while cutting obvious titleless structured fragments earlier.
