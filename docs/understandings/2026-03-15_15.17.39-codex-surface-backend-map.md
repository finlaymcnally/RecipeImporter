# Codex surface/backend map

- Canonical source of truth for the five surfaces is `cookimport/config/codex_decision.py::classify_codex_surfaces`, which enumerates `recipe`, `line_role`, `knowledge`, `tags`, and `prelabel`.
- Backend split:
  - CodexFarm-backed: `recipe`, `knowledge`, `tags`
  - Direct local `codex exec`: `line_role`, `prelabel`
- Evidence:
  - direct `codex exec` helper is `cookimport/llm/codex_exec.py`, used by `cookimport/parsing/canonical_line_roles.py` and `cookimport/labelstudio/prelabel.py`
  - CodexFarm execution uses `SubprocessCodexFarmRunner`, instantiated by `cookimport/llm/codex_farm_orchestrator.py`, `cookimport/llm/codex_farm_knowledge_orchestrator.py`, and `cookimport/tagging/codex_farm_tags_provider.py`
- Important counting nuance: the two recipe pipeline IDs (`codex-farm-3pass-v1` and `codex-farm-2stage-repair-v1`) are one surface with two variants, not two separate backend families.
