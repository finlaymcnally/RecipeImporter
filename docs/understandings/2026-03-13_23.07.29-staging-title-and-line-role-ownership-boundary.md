---
summary: "Title-promotion guardrails in this repo span three modules: stage evidence labeling, late draft title projection, and canonical line-role ownership vetoes."
read_when:
  - When fixing false recipe-title promotion or section-header overwrite bugs
  - When deciding where Codex line-role disagreements should be vetoed versus accepted
---

`cookimport/staging/stage_block_predictions.py` only controls deterministic benchmark/stage evidence labels, so it can stop false `RECIPE_TITLE` or `RECIPE_VARIANT` promotion in stage artifacts but it does not prevent late recipe-name overwrite. That overwrite happens in `cookimport/staging/draft_v1.py` inside `apply_line_role_spans_to_recipes`, which is where projected line-role spans can replace `recipe.name`.

Syntax-heavy line-role ownership is enforced earlier, in `cookimport/parsing/canonical_line_roles.py`, after deterministic and Codex predictions are sanitized. That is the right boundary for vetoing Codex disagreements on strong `TIME_LINE`, `YIELD_LINE`, `RECIPE_NOTES`, `HOWTO_SECTION`, and ingredient calls while still tagging accepted Codex overrides for later debugging.
