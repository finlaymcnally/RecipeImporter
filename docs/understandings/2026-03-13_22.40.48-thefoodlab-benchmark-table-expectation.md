- The Food Lab benchmark runs inspected under `data/golden/benchmark-vs-golden` all recorded `table_extraction: off` in their run manifests, including Codex benchmark runs with pass4 knowledge enabled.
- Because of that, no `tables/<workbook>/tables.jsonl`, `tables.md`, or non-null pass4 `table_hint` data should be expected from those saved runs.
- Separate from the config issue, the extracted text around the conversion section is already flattened into giant single lines, e.g. `COMMON INGREDIENTS BY VOLUME AND MASS*` followed by one long block containing all rows/cells, and `COMMON WEIGHT CONVERSIONS` collapsed into one line of alternating values.
- Practical implication: rerunning those books with `--table-extraction on` is necessary to test the feature, but may still produce empty table detection for some Food Lab tables until upstream extraction preserves row/column boundaries better.
